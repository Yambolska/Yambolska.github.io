About Me

I’m driven by curiosity about how data reflects real human behavior. I get excited by the relationship between numbers and real-world actions — the idea that behind every metric, there is a decision, a habit, a motivation.

I’ve learned that data doesn’t always tell the full story: patterns can highlight what is happening, but truly understanding why often requires looking beyond the dataset and into real life context.

I’m particularly interested in working on anything that improves user experience — understanding how users interact with products, where they struggle, how they engage, and how products can better serve them.
